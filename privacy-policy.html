Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how ImageCompressor handles user data.

Image Processing

All image compression is performed directly in your browser. Your images are not uploaded, stored or shared with any server.

Information Collection

We do not collect personal information such as name, email or address. Basic anonymous analytics data (such as page visits) may be collected to improve the service.

Cookies

Cookies may be used for basic functionality and analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Third-party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense in the future. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us through the website.
