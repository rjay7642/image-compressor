About ImageCompressor

ImageCompressor is a free online tool designed to help users reduce the file size of images quickly and easily, without compromising visual quality.

Our goal is to provide a simple, fast and privacy-friendly solution for compressing JPG, PNG and WebP images directly in the browser. No files are uploaded to any server – everything is processed locally on your device.

Why we built this tool

Large image files slow down websites, consume more data and affect user experience. By optimizing images, you can improve website speed, SEO performance and save storage space.

Our mission

To make image optimization accessible to everyone – developers, students, bloggers and businesses – through a clean, secure and easy-to-use web tool.
